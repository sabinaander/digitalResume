ABOUT ME

Hi!
My name is Sabina Andersson, I'm 30 years old and a student at Medieinstitutet Gothenburg studying the field of Front End Web Development.
I'm expected to finish my studies in June 2023 with a total of 2 LIA periods in Autumn 2022 and Spring 2023.

I'm Swedish and lives in the heart of gotheburg, majorna, with my partner and 2 cats. In my spare time my main interests are gaming, twitch streaming, mountain biking, beer (I attend lots of festivals, obscure beer gatherings and sometimes brew my own beer), photography, coding and digital work.

I'm currently trying to learn more about Adobe illustrator, vector graphics and video editing via adobe premier, besides new programming languages.

A dream workplace for me would be a hbtq-positive creative collective with possibilities to work in several areas, both coding, photography, graphic design and other fun outlets to evolve and find new solutions.

I'm 100% one of those "bounce balls" that can't sit still, nor stop creating. I often get hyper focused on projects, and am obsessed with learning new things. You'll probably find me sitting by a computer for 12 hours on end with my neon headphones listening to 90's techno being absolutely happy with grinding new knowledge.

I chose to write this page in English since I know there's a huge portion of multilingual workplaces out there.
I speak Swedish as a main language, but I communicate in English very often! :)
